About Me

I was born in Medan on October 30, 1997. I graduated in 2019 with a diploma in Civil Aviation and Safety Engineering (Air Traffic Control) and successfully earned my ATC Licence. At that time, there was no recruitment opportunity available, so I continued my professional development by pursuing a Surveillance Rating from November 2019 until February 2020. In August 2020, I decided to continue my education by enrolling in a Bachelor’s degree program in Business Administration at Terbuka University, which I successfully completed at the end of 2023. In April 2020, AirNav Indonesia opened a recruitment program, and I submitted my application. However, by the end of April 2020, I received an email stating that the recruitment process would be postponed until further notice due to the COVID-19 pandemic. I strongly believe that life must go on and that continuous learning is essential. In May 2021, I joined a course on Front-End Development for Beginners offered by the Digital Talent Scholarship (Kominfo).

I earned the AWS Cloud Foundation certification from Dicoding and completed several online trainings, including Personal Branding, Interpersonal Skills (by the Ministry of Communication and Informatics), and Front-End Web Development for Beginners (by Dicoding). Starting from September 2023 until now, I founded and managed a mobile technology business that focuses on professional smartphone repair services, prepaid credit (top-up), internet package sales, and smartphone trade-ins. This entrepreneurial journey has not only sharpened my technical troubleshooting abilities but also enhanced my customer service, business management, and problem-solving skills. Managing this business allowed me to combine my interest in technology with real-world practice, giving me valuable hands-on experience in providing solutions and adapting to customer needs. Through this diverse journey—from aviation studies to technology learning and entrepreneurship—I have built a strong mindset of adaptability, continuous growth, and innovation. I am eager to bring this spirit to Apple Academy, where I can further develop my skills as a developer, collaborate with like-minded innovators, and contribute to creating impactful digital solutions.
